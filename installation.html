THE CENTRE PIECE

The TV screen displays a real-time silhouette made of words—words that describe identity, perception, and the self we present to the world.

The acrylic panels in front filter and fragment your view, creating layers of color and meaning.

LEFT WALL

Your webcam silhouette is reconstructed from thousands of particles. Your body, face, and gestures are formed from light.

You are made visible through data. Move, and watch yourself dissolve and reform in real time.

RIGHT WALL

Through the webcam, your silhouette is captured and inverted. Particles fill the background around you, defining you by absence.

You appear as a void carved from light. The space you occupy becomes emptiness, and what surrounds you defines your form.
