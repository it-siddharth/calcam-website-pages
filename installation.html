THE CENTRE PIECE

The TV screen displays a real-time silhouette made of words—words that describe identity, perception, and the self we present to the world.

The acrylic panels in front filter and fragment your view, creating layers of color and meaning.

LEFT WALL

A particle projection dances on this wall, forming a human silhouette from thousands of points of light.

It represents the fragmented nature of digital identity—how we exist as data points in the modern world.

RIGHT WALL

The opposite perspective. A mirror to the projection, this wall holds the space where your shadow would fall.

In the contrast between light and dark, we find the boundaries of self.
