CALCULATED CAMOUFLAGE

This website is the digital documentation of my art installation titled "Calculated Camouflage". This installation was done as part of my grad thesis at MICA, Baltimore.
Through an interactive installation of light and space, viewers explore how their authentic identity flows through every version of themselves.
